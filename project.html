Rules :-
1. Two teams, each with 11 players.
2. The batting team sends two batsmen onto the field.
3. The fielding team sends one bowler to deliver the ball.
4. The batsmen try to score runs by hitting the ball and running.
5. The fielding team tries to get the batsmen out.
